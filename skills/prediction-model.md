# Prediction Model Skill

Use a rule-based model for MVP.

Base Life Expectancy

78 years

Adjustments

Smoking
-8 years

Regular exercise
+3 years

Good sleep
+2 years

Obesity
-5 years

Healthy diet
+2 years

Alcohol abuse
-3 years

Prediction Formula

predicted_lifespan =
base_life_expectancy
+ adjustments

Death Date

death_date =
birth_date + predicted_lifespan

Days Remaining

days_remaining =
death_date - current_date
